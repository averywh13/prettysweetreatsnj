MEET THE OWNER

Paige Weksler-Hart is a passionate small business owner based in the heart of New Jersey. In 2020, she combined her love for animals with her entrepreneurial spirit to launch a unique business that specializes in crafting high-quality, all-natural treats for horses and dogs. While managing her growing business, Paige also excels in her academic studies at Rutgers University, where she is building a strong foundation in business and finance.
